    Samsung Engineering receives two contracts worth $ 500 million USD from Thailand

    Samsung Engineering announced that it received two contracts from two subsidiaries of PTT, a Thailand state-owned oil & gas company. The contracts which have a combined value of approximately $ 500 million USD are awarded for a petrochemical project and a power generation project located in Rayong, 150km southeast of Bangkok.
